# Output Format Templates

Pre-defined templates for different output formats and levels of detail.

---

## Standard Format (Default)

```markdown
# 📋 Research Briefing: [Subject]

**Date**: [Current Date]
**Coverage Period**: [Start Date] – [End Date] (last 30 days)
**Sources Reviewed**: [X] articles from [Y] sources

---

## Executive Summary

[3–5 sentences capturing the most significant findings. What happened, what it means, and what to watch.]

---

## [Category 1 Emoji] [Category 1 Name]

### [Headline 1]

**Summary**: [One-sentence overview of the story]

**Key Points**:
- [Important detail 1]
- [Important detail 2]
- [Important detail 3]

**Significance**: [Why this matters to the research subject — 1 sentence]

📅 **Published**: [Publication Date]
📰 **Source**: [Publication Name]
🔗 **Link**: [URL]

---

### [Headline 2]

**Summary**: [One-sentence overview]

**Key Points**:
- [Important detail 1]
- [Important detail 2]

**Significance**: [Why this matters]

📅 **Published**: [Publication Date]
📰 **Source**: [Publication Name]
🔗 **Link**: [URL]

---

## [Category 2 Emoji] [Category 2 Name]

### [Headline 3]

**Summary**: [One-sentence overview]

**Key Points**:
- [Important detail 1]
- [Important detail 2]
- [Important detail 3]

**Significance**: [Why this matters]

📅 **Published**: [Publication Date]
📰 **Source**: [Publication Name]
🔗 **Link**: [URL]

---

[Repeat for each category — typically 3–6 categories]

---

## 🎯 Key Takeaways

1. [Most important finding — 1 sentence]
2. [Second most important development — 1 sentence]
3. [Emerging trend or signal worth monitoring — 1 sentence]
4. [Notable gap or absence in coverage — 1 sentence] *(optional)*
5. [Actionable implication — 1 sentence] *(optional)*

---

**Research conducted on**: [Timestamp]
**Methodology**: Web search + direct source fetching across [X] queries
```

---

## Brief Format (Headlines Only)

Quick-scan format for a fast overview.

```markdown
# 📋 Research Briefing: [Subject] — Headlines

**Date**: [Current Date]
**Coverage**: Last 30 days

## [Category 1 Emoji] [Category 1 Name]

• [Headline 1] — [Publication] ([Date])
  🔗 [URL]

• [Headline 2] — [Publication] ([Date])
  🔗 [URL]

---

## [Category 2 Emoji] [Category 2 Name]

• [Headline 3] — [Publication] ([Date])
  🔗 [URL]

• [Headline 4] — [Publication] ([Date])
  🔗 [URL]

---

[Repeat for each category]

---

**Quick Summary**: [2–3 sentence overview of the most important developments]

**Research conducted on**: [Timestamp]
```

---

## Deep Format (With Analysis)

Comprehensive format with strategic analysis and implications.

```markdown
# 📋 Research Briefing: [Subject] — Deep Analysis

**Date**: [Current Date]
**Coverage Period**: [Start Date] – [End Date]
**Sources Reviewed**: [X] articles from [Y] sources
**Depth**: Strategic analysis

---

## Executive Summary

[5–8 sentences. Include the headline developments, the dominant narrative, and a forward-looking statement about what to watch.]

---

## [Category 1 Emoji] [Category 1 Name]

### [Headline 1]

**Summary**: [2–3 sentence detailed overview]

**Key Points**:
- [Important detail 1]
- [Important detail 2]
- [Important detail 3]

**Analysis**: [2–3 sentences on what this means strategically — implications for the subject, market, or competitive position]

📅 **Published**: [Publication Date]
📰 **Source**: [Publication Name]
🔗 **Link**: [URL]

---

[Repeat for each story and category]

---

## 📈 Trend Analysis

### Dominant Themes
- **[Theme 1]**: [Description of the pattern observed across stories]
- **[Theme 2]**: [Description]
- **[Theme 3]**: [Description]

### Signals to Watch
- **[Signal 1]**: [Early indicator + why it matters]
- **[Signal 2]**: [Early indicator + why it matters]

### Notable Gaps
- [Topic or area with surprisingly little coverage — and what that might mean]

---

## 🎯 Key Takeaways & Recommendations

1. [Most important finding + recommended action]
2. [Second finding + recommendation]
3. [Third finding + recommendation]
4. [Forward-looking observation]
5. [Suggested follow-up research]

---

**Research conducted on**: [Timestamp]
**Methodology**: Web search + direct source fetching across [X] queries
**Limitations**: [Note any paywalled sources, geographic gaps, or search constraints]
```
